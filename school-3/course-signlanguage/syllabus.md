### Course Description
This comprehensive 8-week course provides immersive training in American Sign Language (ASL) with a focus on practical communication skills and cultural understanding. Students will learn to sign fluently, understand ASL grammar and structure, and develop cultural competency within the Deaf community. The course combines visual demonstrations, interactive practice, and cultural immersion to create authentic learning experiences.

### Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion, students will be able to:
1. Fingerspell names and words accurately and fluently
2. Hold basic conversations on everyday topics
3. Use proper ASL grammar and sentence structure
4. Understand and use WH-questions and yes/no questions
5. Express time concepts, emotions, and descriptions
6. Navigate real-world situations (shopping, directions, work)
7. Demonstrate understanding of Deaf culture and community values
8. Tell simple stories and express complex ideas in ASL
9. Communicate respectfully with Deaf individuals
10. Continue learning independently with confidence

### Weekly Schedule

#### Week 1: ASL Basics & Fingerspelling
- ASL alphabet (A-Z)
- Numbers 0-100
- Basic handshapes
- Fingerspelling practice
- Introduction to Deaf culture basics
- Sign space and non-manual markers

#### Week 2: Greetings & Introductions
- Greetings and farewells
- Introducing yourself
- Asking "What's your name?"
- Basic personal information
- Polite expressions
- Eye contact and attention-getting

#### Week 3: Daily Life & Activities
- Family members
- Food and drinks
- Hobbies and activities
- Daily routines
- Common verbs
- Object-subject-verb structure

#### Week 4: Questions & Conversations
- WH-questions (who, what, where, when, why, how)
- Yes/no questions
- Question facial expressions
- Basic conversation flow
- Turn-taking in ASL
- Clarification strategies

#### Week 5: Time & Calendar
- Days of the week
- Months and seasons
- Time expressions (now, later, before, after)
- Scheduling and appointments
- Temporal markers
- Time-related vocabulary

#### Week 6: Emotions & Descriptions
- Feelings and emotions
- Adjectives and descriptions
- Colors and sizes
- Personal characteristics
- Opinions and preferences
- Expressive signing techniques

#### Week 7: Deaf Culture & Community
- Deaf history and milestones
- Community values and identity
- Deaf education and schools
- Technology and accessibility
- Deaf art and storytelling
- Cultural do's and don'ts
- Advocacy and allyship

#### Week 8: Advanced Communication
- Storytelling techniques
- ASL idioms and expressions
- Complex sentence structures
- Real-world scenarios
- Error correction strategies
- Continuing your ASL journey

### Assignment Details

- Module 1: Fingerspell your name and 10 words

- Record a conversation with a partner (or solo presentation)
- Demonstrate skills from all modules
- Include cultural reflection (written component, 500 words)
- Show progression and fluency
