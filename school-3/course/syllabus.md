### Course Description
This intensive 8-week course provides comprehensive training in critical thinking, logical reasoning, and evidence evaluation. Students will learn to identify, analyze, and construct arguments while avoiding common cognitive biases and logical fallacies. The course combines theoretical foundations with practical applications across various domains including science, business, and everyday decision-making.

### Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion, students will be able to:
1. Formalize complex arguments into premises and conclusions
2. Identify and avoid 20+ common logical fallacies
3. Recognize and mitigate 15+ cognitive biases
4. Apply probabilistic reasoning to uncertain situations
5. Evaluate evidence quality across different domains
6. Construct robust, defensible arguments
7. Use decision-making frameworks for better choices
8. Communicate reasoning clearly in writing and speech
