### Course Description
This transformative 8-week course explores human psychology through the lenses of theology, modern psychology, and compassionate understanding. Students will learn about narcissism, human nature, and how to maintain optimistic perspectives while staying grounded in reality. The course combines ancient wisdom traditions with contemporary psychological insights to help students understand themselves and others with greater clarity and compassion.

### Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion, students will be able to:
1. Understand key concepts in human psychology and consciousness
2. Explore theological perspectives on human nature and purpose
3. Recognize narcissistic patterns with compassion and clarity
4. Cultivate realistic optimism and resilience
5. Develop greater self-awareness and emotional intelligence
6. Set healthy boundaries while maintaining compassion
7. Integrate spiritual practices into daily life
8. Apply understanding to improve relationships and personal growth

### Weekly Schedule

#### Week 1: The Human Mind: An Introduction
- Understanding consciousness and self-awareness
- Mental frameworks and cognitive patterns
- The relationship between mind, body, and spirit
- Introduction to compassionate observation

#### Week 2: Theology & Human Nature
- Spiritual perspectives on human nature
- Purpose, meaning, and the human condition
- Comparing theological views across traditions
- Integrating faith and psychology

#### Week 3: Narcissism: Understanding the Spectrum
- What is narcissism? (clinical and everyday)
- The spectrum: healthy self-esteem to pathological narcissism
- Recognizing patterns with compassion
- Understanding causes and contexts

#### Week 4: The Optimist's Mindset
- Realistic optimism vs. blind positivity
- Resilience and growth mindset
- Finding meaning in challenges
- Cultivating hope without denial

#### Week 5: Self-Awareness & Growth
- Emotional intelligence fundamentals
- Introspection and self-reflection practices
- Recognizing your own patterns
- Growth through awareness

#### Week 6: Relationships & Boundaries
- Healthy vs. unhealthy relationship patterns
- Setting boundaries with compassion
- Protecting yourself while staying loving
- Communication skills for difficult situations

#### Week 7: Spiritual Practices & Mindfulness
- Meditation and contemplative practices
- Prayer and connection practices
- Mindfulness in daily life
- Integrating wisdom traditions

#### Week 8: Integration & Transformation
- Bringing concepts together
- Creating your personal growth plan
- Maintaining practices long-term
- Final project presentation
