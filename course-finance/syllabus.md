### Course Description
This comprehensive 8-week course provides practical training in personal finance management and investing fundamentals. Students will learn to budget effectively, manage debt, understand credit, build savings, and begin investing for long-term wealth. The course combines financial theory with actionable strategies and real-world applications.

### Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion, students will be able to:
1. Create and maintain a personal budget
2. Understand and improve credit scores
3. Develop debt payoff strategies
4. Evaluate banking and savings options
5. Understand investment basics and risk/return
6. Build a diversified investment portfolio
7. Plan for retirement effectively
8. Optimize taxes and create a comprehensive financial plan
