Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

School 2 ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our learning platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address
  • Profile Information: Bio, profile picture URL, social media links
  • Learning Data: Course progress, quiz scores, completed modules
  • Certificates: Generated certificates and achievements

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Usage data and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our learning platform
  • Track your learning progress
  • Generate certificates
  • Enable social features (friends, profiles)
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send important updates (if you opt-in)

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not transmit your data to external servers unless you explicitly share it (e.g., sharing certificates).

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing

6. Data Export

You can export all your data at any time through your profile settings. The export includes:

  • Profile information
  • Course progress
  • Quiz scores
  • Certificates
  • Friends list

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through your profile settings. This action is irreversible.

8. Cookies

We use localStorage (similar to cookies) to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Store your preferences (theme, layout)
  • Track your progress

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • CDN Services: For loading libraries (Marked.js, Highlight.js)
  • Analytics: Privacy-friendly analytics (if enabled)

10. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • Input sanitization to prevent XSS attacks
  • URL validation for all external links
  • HTTPS encryption in production

11. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for educational purposes. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through your profile settings or email.

14. Data Protection Officer

For GDPR-related inquiries, you can contact our Data Protection Officer through the platform.
